ABOUT GIR NATIONAL PARK

Located Around 43kms From Somnath And 60kms From Junagadh, Gir National Park Is One Of The Major Attraction Of Gujarat. Gir National Park In Gujarat Is The Only Place In India Having The Largest Population Of Asiatic Lions. Spread Across Nearly 1412 Sq Km Of Land. There Is Also A Huge Population Of Wild Boar, Sambar Deer, Spotted Deer, Nilgai, And Carnivorous Creatures, Including Hyena, Wild Cat, Fox And Leopard, And Further Than 300 Species. One Of The Most Beautiful Public Premises In The Gujarat, Gir National Park Has A Distinctive Geography Comprising Of Insulated Hills And Rugged Ridges. However, You Can Plan On Visiting Gir National Park In Gujarat, If You're Looking For A Demesne With Instigative Wildlife And Natural Beauty.
